Project Description: Drop Test Simulation

The "Drop Test Simulation" project was undertaken to evaluate the structural integrity and impact resistance of a product when subjected to accidental drops during handling or transportation...

The drop test scenario was designed based on realistic conditions, including drop height, orientation, and target surface material properties...

Meshing was a critical step, where an appropriate element size was selected to balance accuracy and computational efficiency...

Post-processing involved evaluating the von Mises stress, plastic deformation, and displacement fields to identify failure-prone areas...

Project Takeaways:

The project demonstrated the effectiveness of simulation-driven design in predicting real-world failure modes and minimizing development cycles...
